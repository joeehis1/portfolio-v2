Web Developer

My journey in web development began in the first quarter of 2022 when I took a leap to turn my passion into a career. Starting with HTML and CSS, I embarked on the exciting path of building website clones and various projects to gain foundational experience.

As I delved into frontend development, I realized the significance of backend integration. To create meaningful projects, I expanded my skills to include Node.js, Express, and MongoDB. Learning how to connect frontend and backend layers became a pivotal point in my journey.

Web development is an ever-evolving field, and I understand the importance of a robust foundation in both frontend and backend technologies.
